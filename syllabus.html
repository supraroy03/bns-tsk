Syllabus Structure

Curriculum Framework

The school strictly follows the SEBA curriculum for Classes IX and X. Internal syllabus planning for lower classes is aligned with SEBA academic standards.

Subject-Wise Structure

English

Grammar, literature, comprehension and writing skills as per prescribed textbook.

Assamese / Hindi (MIL)

Language development, grammar, prose and poetry.

General Mathematics

Arithmetic, algebra, geometry and mensuration as per SEBA guidelines.

General Science

Physics, Chemistry and Biology fundamentals aligned with SEBA syllabus.

Social Science

History, Geography, Political Science and Economics.

Computer / IP

Basic computer applications and digital literacy.
