Academic Excellence

Curriculum & Examination Framework

A structured SEBA-aligned curriculum ensuring conceptual clarity, disciplined scholarship and progressive academic development.

Curriculum Structure

Systematically aligned with SEBA standards for senior classes while building strong academic foundations in the primary and middle sections.

The institution adheres strictly to the SEBA curriculum for Classes IX and X, preparing students for the HSLC Examination. Foundational and middle sections progressively cultivate analytical reasoning, language fluency and subject mastery to ensure seamless academic transition into secondary education.

Subject Framework

Comprehensive academic coverage across linguistic, scientific and analytical disciplines.

English

Literature, grammar, comprehension and structured composition aligned with SEBA guidelines.

Assamese / Hindi (MIL)

Language proficiency, grammar, prose and poetry studies fostering linguistic confidence.

General Mathematics

Algebra, geometry, arithmetic and applied analytical problem-solving techniques.

General Science

Integrated foundations of Physics, Chemistry and Biology promoting scientific inquiry.

Social Science

History, Geography, Political Science and Economics developing civic and analytical awareness.

Computer Studies

Digital literacy, application-based learning and introductory programming fundamentals.
