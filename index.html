About the Institution

A legacy of structured education and principled growth.

Founded in 1970, Babies Nursery Co-ed High School is affiliated to the Board of Secondary Education, Assam (SEBA). We provide comprehensive English medium education from foundational early learning to secondary level.

Our pedagogical framework integrates academic excellence with ethical development, ensuring students emerge as responsible, capable and socially conscious citizens.
